Nerium Oleander

الدفلى

Basic Information

Nerium Oleander is a popular ornamental shrub known for its beautiful, vibrant flowers and ability to thrive in hot, dry climates. It is widely used in landscapes, especially in central regions of Saudi Arabia.

  • Type: Shrub
  • Lifespan: 8–12 years
  • Height: Can reach up to 4 meters
  • Blooming Season: Spring to Fall

Care & Benefits

Oleander is relatively easy to care for and requires minimal maintenance once established. It provides dense coverage, making it excellent for hedges and privacy screens.

  • Care Level: Easy
  • Benefits: Decorative appeal, privacy barrier, drought tolerance
  • Caution: All parts of the plant are toxic if ingested
  • Historical Use: Used historically for medicinal properties but highly toxic.

Growing Conditions

Sunlight: Full Sun

Climate: Hot and Dry

Temperature Tolerance: Up to 45°C, Frost-sensitive

Soil: Sandy, Well-drained

Placement: Outdoor Only

Watering: Moderate (Let soil dry between waterings)

Medicinal & Toxic Properties

Despite its beauty, Nerium Oleander contains toxins harmful to humans and animals. It was used in ancient folk medicine but should be handled with caution today.

  • Toxicity: Highly toxic (especially if ingested)
  • Traditional Uses: Used historically in traditional medicine under strict supervision.
